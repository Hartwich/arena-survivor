# Third-party assets

## Ironbound Dungeon

The selectable `ironbound-dungeon` visual theme uses cropped and resized PNG derivatives from the following public-domain sources. The original source sheets are not shipped; only the runtime-ready derivatives required by Arena Survivor are included.

### Dungeon Pack

- Creator: natomarcacini (`nato` on OpenGameArt)
- Source: https://opengameart.org/content/dungeon-pack
- License: Creative Commons Zero 1.0 (CC0)
- Used for: 13 character visuals, 7 enemy families, 22 item icons, three weapon visuals, and the arena floor
- Source resolution: most sprites are 256 x 256 pixels

### Ancient Armory

- Creator: JoeRaig
- Source: https://opengameart.org/content/ancient-armory-2d-historical-weapons
- License: Creative Commons Zero 1.0 (CC0)
- Used for: 18 vector-style weapon visuals

Both source pages explicitly permit copying, modification, redistribution, and commercial use without attribution. Attribution is retained here for provenance.
